fsck_hfs for FreeBSD

Status

fsck_hfs is based on the corresponding utility from Darwin 6.6 (MacOS 10.2.6.)

Unlike the original fsck_hfs from Darwin, this version works on little-endian architectures, as well. Its filesystem verification and repair functionalities have been tested on i386.

Known Problems and Limitations

Only devices with the block size of 512 bytes are supported.

Plans for Improvement

Currently none.
